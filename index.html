About Me

Hey, everyone! My name is Ben Minahan and I've been playing music in one form or another my whole life. Guitar, bass, piano, voice, and more recently drums and banjo. I also LOVE making beats, arranging melodies, or making weird AF sounds in my home audio production studio in Kansas City, MO. My main goal with music is to make songs that people love listening to, and have fun doing it.

​

Over the years, I have had the opportunity to play a wide range of music with some extremely talented musicians. But, I am always cooking something up solo. Since 2020, I have released 16 original songs in a variety of genres.

​

In 2023, I'm hoping to get into playing more live shows, so I hope to see you there! Until then, I invite you to listen to all my music for free (on YouTube or all major streaming platforms), and if you like what you hear, you can share it with others or even buy a physical CD like its 2002!

​

My latest record, "K.O." will be available everywhere March 1st, 2023.

​

Cheers,

​
